long_description: |
  The Karangahake Hall Market is held on the fourth Sunday of each month from 9 am to 1 pm in the historic hall on State Highway 2. Surrounded by the gorge’s bush and heritage trails, the market offers a selection of plants, seedlings, fresh produce, free‑range eggs, honey, jams, baking and handcrafted items. It provides a community meeting point for residents of the Karangahake Gorge and nearby towns, and supports small producers in the region.

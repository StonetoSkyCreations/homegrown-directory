  Earth Store is a combined deli, café and wholefood shop on Albert Street in Whitianga. The store offers organic dry goods, fresh fruit and vegetables, herbs and spices, and a range of natural health and eco household products. Its café serves organic coffee, smoothies, freshly made cabinet items, soups and light meals, with vegetarian and gluten‑free options. Shoppers can pick up locally grown produce and browse a selection of dairy alternatives, nut butters and superfoods. The friendly team are passionate about sustainable living and support local growers by stocking seasonal, spray‑free produce whenever possible. Opening hours run early in the morning for commuters through to late afternoon.

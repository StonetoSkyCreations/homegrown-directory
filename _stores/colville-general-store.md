long_description: |
  The Colville General Store has operated as a community‑owned cooperative since 1978 and remains the last grocery stop before the remote northern Coromandel. It stocks a wide range of goods including fresh fruit and vegetables, bulk grains, flours, herbs and spices, wholefoods, dried goods and locally made preserves. Customers will also find bread, milk, meat, organic and gluten‑free options, fuel and hardware supplies, making it a one‑stop shop for locals, campers and travellers. The store prioritises environmentally friendly and organic products where possible and provides friendly advice and account services for regular customers.

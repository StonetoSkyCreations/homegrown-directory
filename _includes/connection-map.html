{%- comment -%} Web-of-connection block for a listing. Computes suppliers (upstream) and outlets (downstream) from sourced_from / supplies_to in both directions, marks reciprocated links verified, and renders: (1) a lazy-loaded Leaflet mini-map of this listing and its geocoded connections with animated ant-path flow lines (assets/js/connections-map.js), and (2) accessible directional chip rows as the always-present textual index and the no-JS / no-coords fallback. Renders nothing when the listing has no connections. Pass entity= (default page). {%- endcomment -%} {%- assign cm_entity = include.entity | default: page -%} {%- assign cm_empty = "" | split: "" -%} {%- assign cm_supplies = cm_entity.supplies_to | default: cm_empty -%} {%- assign cm_sourced = cm_entity.sourced_from | default: cm_empty -%} {%- assign cm_all = site.farms | concat: site.markets | concat: site.stores | concat: site.restaurants | concat: site.distributors | concat: site.vendors -%} {%- assign cm_out_a = cm_all | where_exp: 'i', 'cm_supplies contains i.slug' -%} {%- assign cm_out_b = cm_all | where_exp: 'i', 'i.sourced_from contains cm_entity.slug' -%} {%- assign cm_outlets = cm_out_a | concat: cm_out_b | uniq | where_exp: 'i', 'i.slug != cm_entity.slug' -%} {%- assign cm_sup_a = cm_all | where_exp: 'i', 'cm_sourced contains i.slug' -%} {%- assign cm_sup_b = cm_all | where_exp: 'i', 'i.supplies_to contains cm_entity.slug' -%} {%- assign cm_suppliers = cm_sup_a | concat: cm_sup_b | uniq | where_exp: 'i', 'i.slug != cm_entity.slug' -%} {%- assign cm_total = cm_suppliers.size | plus: cm_outlets.size -%} {%- if cm_total > 0 -%}
